Our Product Range

Industrial-Grade Minerals

From raw silica sand to ultra-fine quartz powder, we offer a comprehensive range of mineral products tailored to your industrial application.

🏖️
Industrial Grade

Silica Sand

High-purity silica sand with consistent grain sizing, ideal for glass manufacturing, foundry casting, and construction applications.

SiO₂ ≥ 99.5% 0.1 – 2.0mm
🔬
Ultra-Fine Grade

Quartz Powder

Micronised quartz powder processed to precise fineness levels for use in coatings, rubber, ceramics, and advanced materials.

SiO₂ ≥ 99.7% D50: 5–50µm
💎
Premium Grade

High-Purity Quartz (HPQ)

Ultra-low impurity quartz for semiconductor, photovoltaic, and optical applications where maximum purity is critical.

SiO₂ ≥ 99.9% Low Fe₂O₃
