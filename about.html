About Pacific Industrial Mineral Exports

A dedicated Australian exporter connecting the world's industries with premium silica and quartz minerals.

Our Mission

Built on Quality and Reliability

Pacific Industrial Mineral Exports (PIME) was founded with a clear purpose: to supply world-class industrial minerals from Australia's rich geological resources to global markets that demand consistent purity and performance.

Australia is home to some of the world's finest silica and quartz deposits. We harness this natural advantage by working with established mining operations across Western Australia, Queensland, New South Wales, and South Australia — applying rigorous quality controls at every stage of production and export.

Whether you require bulk industrial silica sand or ultra-fine high-purity quartz powder, PIME delivers on specification, on time, and with transparent communication throughout the supply chain.

Sourcing

Australia's Premier Mineral Regions

We source from established deposits across key Australian mineral-producing states, ensuring consistent supply and quality.

🌅

Western Australia

Home to extensive silica sand deposits along the coast and inland, WA is Australia's largest silica-producing state with world-class purity profiles.

☀️

Queensland

Queensland's quartz-rich geological formations yield high-quality material suited for glass, electronics, and industrial chemical applications.

🏙️

New South Wales

NSW deposits offer consistent silica sources for construction, foundry, and filtration markets with convenient port access for export.

🌾

South Australia

SA silica resources complement our product range with additional grades suited to specialised industrial uses and custom mineral blends.
